About AGENSTOCK

AGENSTOCK is an AI-powered conversational stock research platform that helps users explore, analyze and generate actionable research reports with intuitive visuals and live simulations.

  • Conversational Research: Ask questions in plain English and get structured, multi-level summaries.
  • Interactive Visual Analytics: Candlesticks, indicators (SMA/RSI/MACD), and live chart interactions.
  • Portfolio Insights: Track holdings and receive AI-driven recommendations and rebalancing tips.
  • Downloadable Reports: Export high-quality PDF research reports with embedded visuals.
  • Live Simulations: Visual-only, client-side simulations that preview the analytics experience.
  • Privacy-first: No admin internals are exposed on this page; this is user-focused.

Architecture Overview

 
Backend (FastAPI)
  ├─ Services: stock_service, research_report_service
  ├─ Endpoints: /api/research/enhanced, /api/research/enhanced-report, /api/research/enhanced-report-pdf
  └─ PDF gen: reportlab + server-side charts

Frontend (Jinja2 + JS)
  ├─ Interactive charts: Plotly.js (client)
  └─ Simulations & demos: canvas/svg

Data Providers: yfinance, AlphaVantage, IndStock (conditional)

Caching: in-memory TTL (swap for Redis in prod)
ASCII architecture for quick reference (convertible to diagram image)

How AGENSTOCK works

1. Data Aggregation

We pull high-quality market data from multiple sources, clean and normalize it for downstream analytics.

2. Signal Processing

Computed indicators (SMA, RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands) and technical overlays provide quick trend signals.

3. AI Summarization

Natural language models synthesize multi-source inputs into concise insights, investment theses, and risk annotations.

4. Interactive Visualization

Explore interactive charts, toggle indicators, zoom and export images — all built for non-technical users.
